TRIPLE TOP PATTERN

A Triple Top Pattern is a drive back figure that exists when the price of the asset reaches a specific resistance level thrice and break down. Basically its technical analysis that forecast a reversal in the asset’s price change. Now the question is whether it is bearish or bullish? Obviously it’s a bearish formation that resembles the declination of the buyer’s strength, and the sellers are beginning to take control of the market.

Triple top pattern has three successive peaks at same price level approximately, with minor withdraws in between. Now it’s a common believe that triple top is an effective trading tool, but that doesn’t guarantee of trend reversal. As a result, traders are likely to technical analysis instruments to validate before taking trading decisions.

SIGNIFICANCE

The Triple top pattern is a significant tool for traders as it shows the important insights into market structure. By observing these patterns, traders can predict potential rend reversals and adjust their trading strategies respectively. It permits the traders to take up short positions or exit the long positions to take advantage on the upcoming downward move.in addition to other technical positions, the triple top pattern can also act as a configuration tool, reinforce the conviction of trend reversal.

HOW TO DETERMINE THE TRIPLE TOP

Traders look for three different peaks formed at same price level, distinguish by two troughs. These peaks are formed when the price touches a specific resistance level thrice and cannot surpass it. The troughs are made when the price reiterates some of its gains from the resistance level.

TRADING STRATEGIES

The moment the traders have recognized this formation, they incorporates with the trading strategies to take best out of it. Nevertheless, there are rulings or strategies that are used as the foundation by the traders:

    Entry
  • When the price below the neckline splits, the traders can enter the short position, joins the two troughs that separate the peaks. The triple top candlestick is confirmed when the critical support level breaks down. It indicates the reversal trends.
    Stop loss strategy
  • The stop loss strategy is placed above the neckline to manage the risk. The stop loss order limits their losses when the price starts to elevate again .According to the theory, the traders can put a stop loss on the neckline. Nevertheless, mostly the price tests again the support level after a breakout in order to rise the early exit.
    Take profit strategy:
  • Profit target have several factors to determine. The common technique is to calculate the distance between the tops and bottoms and incorporate it with breakout point.

TO SUMMARISE

Triple top is powerful tool in technical analysis that helps to predict reversals in price movements of a trading asset. It is useful to recognize potential trends and grab the market opportunities. However, these need a keen identification to understand and confirm the strategies to get good profit out from it.

Market conditions, News events, and other elements can affect the asset’s price before taking the asset price position. Traders use and implement these strategies when they are confident to take trading decisions. Ensure to have risk management and adopt your strategies based on the market conditions.
